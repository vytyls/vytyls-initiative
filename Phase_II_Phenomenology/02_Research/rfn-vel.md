# RFN - VEL - Veil of Entangled Luminance

The **Veil of Entangled Luminance (VEL)** is a **specific Symbolic Echo Set (SES)** described as a **white, threadlike, hyper-dense structure** encountered at the **edge of perception**, particularly between the **Synchronic Layer (L4)** and the **Extra-Perceptual Layer (L5)**. It resembles a **spectral mycelium or filament lattice** and is strongly associated with **non-retinal perception** and **spatial proprioception**. This veil appears as a **semi-visual, semi-tactile interface**, radiating both symbolic density and sensory information. Functionally, it may signify a **transitional boundary where symbolic compression precedes expanded cognitive field access**. The Veil of Entangled Luminance is also explicitly identified as a **transductive veil** or **liminal compression mesh**.
